Last Updated: January 2025 · Canada Marketplace Inc.

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the website remember your preferences and keep you logged in.

3. What We Don't Use

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking cookies, analytics cookies, or any cookies that follow you across other websites.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies will prevent you from logging in and using the Service.
