Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025 · Version 1.0 · Canada Marketplace Inc.

1. Who We Are

Canada Marketplace Inc. ("we", "us", "our") operates www.canadamarketplace.ca. We are a federally incorporated Canadian corporation.

Privacy Officer: privacy@canadamarketplace.ca

Mailing Address: Available upon request via email.

2. What We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to operate the marketplace:

  • Account: Email address, username, province, city
  • Selling: Government ID (processed by Stripe, never stored on our servers), bank account details (processed by Stripe only)
  • Transactions: Shipping address (shared only with seller and shipping carrier), payment information (processed by Stripe, PCI-DSS compliant)
  • Messages: Content of buyer-seller conversations
  • Technical: IP address, browser type, device type (retained 90 days for security)
  • Cookies: Session cookie, preference cookies (see Cookie Policy)

We do NOT collect: Social Insurance Numbers, date of birth, health information, biometric data, or any data beyond what the marketplace requires.

4. Who Sees Your Data

  • Sellers see buyer's shipping name and address (to fulfill orders) and message content
  • Stripe processes all payments and seller verification (PCI-DSS Level 1 certified)
  • Shipping carriers (e.g., Canada Post) receive shipping label data
  • Supabase hosts our database (Canada region)

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for marketing purposes.

5. Where Data Is Stored

All data is stored in Canada. Our database is hosted in the ca-central-1 (Canada Central) region. Your data does not cross international borders.

Sub-processors: Supabase (database, Canada), Stripe (payments, Canada-compliant), Cloudflare R2 (images, Canada-available). No sub-processor stores data outside of Canada.

6. How Long We Keep Data

  • Active accounts: As long as the account exists
  • Deleted accounts: Fully purged within 30 days (except what law requires)
  • Transaction records: 7 years (CRA tax requirement)
  • Server logs (IP addresses): 90 days
  • Inactive accounts: Deletion notice after 2 years of inactivity; deleted 30 days after notice
  • Messages: 2 years after last activity, then deleted

7. Your Rights Under PIPEDA

You have the following rights under the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA):

  • Right of Access: Request all personal data we hold about you. Free of charge. Within 30 days. Email privacy@canadamarketplace.ca.
  • Right to Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data. We will correct it within 30 days or explain why we cannot.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for non-essential data processing. Note: some data (email, shipping address) is required to complete transactions.
  • Right to Complain: File a complaint with us at privacy@canadamarketplace.ca. You may also file a complaint with the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada.
  • Right to Challenge: Challenge the accuracy or completeness of your personal information.

To exercise any right: email privacy@canadamarketplace.ca with your registered email address and the right you wish to exercise. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3)
  • All payment data processed by Stripe (PCI-DSS Level 1)
  • Access controls and audit logging
  • Regular security reviews

9. Data Breaches

In the event of a data breach that poses a real risk of significant harm to individuals, we will:

  1. Notify affected individuals within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach
  2. Report to the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada
  3. Notify any relevant provincial privacy authorities
  4. Take steps to contain and remediate the breach

As required under the PIPEDA Breach of Security Safeguards Regulations (SOR/2018-137).

10. Quebec Law 25

For users in Quebec, additional rights apply under Quebec's Act Respecting the Protection of Personal Information in the Private Sector (Law 25):

  • Right to data portability
  • Right to deletion (with limited exceptions)
  • Right to be informed of automated decision-making
  • Complaints may also be filed with the Commission d'accès à l'information du Québec (CAI)

Our designated person responsible for protection of personal information can be reached at privacy@canadamarketplace.ca.

11. Children

The minimum age to use Canada Marketplace is 16 years, or the age of majority in the user's province of residence, whichever is lower. We do not knowingly collect data from minors below this age.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be notified via email to registered users at least 30 days before taking effect. The version number and date at the top of this page will be updated. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
