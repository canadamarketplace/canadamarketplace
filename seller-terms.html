Seller Agreement

Last Updated: January 2025 · Version 1.0 · Canada Marketplace Inc.

1. Becoming a Seller

To sell on Canada Marketplace, you must: create an account, complete identity verification (government-issued ID via Stripe), connect a Canadian bank account via Stripe Connect, and agree to this Seller Agreement. You must be a resident of Canada.

2. Listing Requirements

  • Title must accurately describe the item
  • Condition must be truthfully stated (New, Like New, Good, Fair)
  • At least 1 clear photo required, max 10 photos
  • Price must be in Canadian dollars ($1 – $100,000)
  • Description must not be misleading
  • Correct category and province must be selected

3. Marketplace Fee

We charge an 8% fee on each completed transaction. This fee is automatically deducted from your payout before it reaches your bank account.

Gold sellers (10+ completed sales, 4.5+ rating) qualify for a reduced 5% fee.

4. Escrow and Payouts

All payments are held in escrow via Stripe Connect until the buyer confirms delivery or the 5-day auto-release period expires. Payouts are initiated automatically and typically arrive in your bank account within 2 business days.

5. Fulfillment Obligations

Shipping: Ship within 3 business days of payment. Provide a valid tracking number. Use appropriate packaging.

Local pickup: Respond to buyer within 24 hours. Complete pickup within 7 days.

6. Taxes

You are responsible for understanding and remitting applicable taxes (GST/HST/PST) on your sales. Canada Marketplace will provide annual sales summaries. If your annual earnings exceed $500 CAD, we will issue a T4A slip as required by the CRA.

7. Prohibited Activities

  • Listing prohibited or illegal items
  • Misrepresenting item condition or authenticity
  • Attempting to transact outside the platform to avoid fees
  • Creating multiple seller accounts
  • Soliciting fake reviews or ratings
  • Using another person's identity or bank account

8. Account Suspension

We may suspend your seller account for: violation of this agreement, fraudulent activity, 3 or more unresolved disputes, failure to ship items, or failure to respond to buyer messages within 48 hours. You may appeal a suspension within 14 days by contacting sellers@canadamarketplace.ca.

9. Liability

You are solely responsible for items you list and ship. Canada Marketplace is not liable for damage during shipping, lost packages, buyer disputes, or any legal issues arising from items you sell. You assume all risk as the seller.

10. Termination

You may close your seller account at any time. Outstanding payouts will be processed. Pending orders must be fulfilled before closure. We may terminate your seller account with 30 days notice for any reason.

11. Contact

Seller Support: sellers@canadamarketplace.ca

Disputes: disputes@canadamarketplace.ca
