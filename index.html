Your Safety First

Built Safe by Design

Every transaction is protected. Your data stays in Canada. We follow the law — not just the minimum.

Escrow Payments

Your money is held safely until you receive and confirm your item. Sellers only get paid after you're satisfied. No exceptions.

Verified Sellers Only

Every seller must verify their government ID and address before listing. No anonymous accounts. No fake sellers.

Data Stays in Canada

All servers are in Canada. Your data never crosses the border. Compliant with PIPEDA and provincial privacy laws including Quebec's Law 25.

Dispute Protection

Item not as described? Never arrived? File a dispute within 14 days. We investigate and resolve — buyer or seller.

Canadian Dollars Only

No USD conversion surprises. No foreign exchange fees. Every price you see is the price you pay — in Canadian dollars.

Full Legal Compliance

PIPEDA compliant. Competition Act compliant. CRA tax reporting ready. Quebec Law 25 ready. We go beyond minimum requirements.
